About Us – LSR Nursery

Welcome to LSR Nursery, one of the trusted plant destinations in the Kadiyam region of Andhra Pradesh. We are passionate about bringing nature closer to homes, gardens, farms, resorts, apartments, and commercial spaces through premium-quality plants and professional landscaping services.

At LSR Nursery, we offer a wide variety of indoor and outdoor plants available for both retail and wholesale supply. Our nursery is known for healthy, well-maintained, and high-quality plants carefully grown with proper care and attention.

We specialize in:

  • Indoor & Outdoor Plants
  • Fruit & Flowering Plants
  • Avenue & Medicinal Plants
  • Decorative Garden Plants
  • Wholesale Supply & Landscaping

Why Customers Choose Us:

  • 1000+ varieties
  • Healthy plants
  • Wholesale prices
  • Expert support

Our expert team provides professional guidance for gardening, plant selection, and landscape development. Whether you are creating a home garden, farm, resort, or commercial outdoor space, LSR Nursery delivers the perfect green solutions.
